About

My Story

I work with clients in UX design to create practical and functional products that offer fabulous experiences for their users. I pride myself on working with clients and listening closely to make sure I meet their goals and needs. This involves creative problem-solving, flexible planning and stunning design, always with the client at the center.

Born and raised in Japan, at 15, I left to attend high school in rural Canada. There, I fell in love with photography and decided to study commercial photography in Toronto, Canada, after which I dove into advertising photography for a decade. Needing a new challenge, I moved to Tokyo where I thrived in this high-octane world for a decade, shooting for clients like Starbucks, McDonald’s, and Triumph International. Self-expression is gold; I always push myself creatively, and I’ve been in 11 solo and group exhibitions.
